About Raj Marketing

Our Story

Established in 2001, Raj Marketing has been serving the people of Mysore with premium quality mosquito nets for over two decades. What started as a small workshop has now grown into one of Mysore's most trusted names for home protection solutions.

Our founder, Madhu, started this journey with a simple mission: to provide every home in Mysore with affordable, durable, and effective mosquito protection. Today, under the management of Govind Raju, we have successfully installed mosquito nets in over 5,000 homes across Mysore city.
